In a perfect world, medicine and medical aid would come cheap to those who need it.

Unfortunately this isn't the case. We've thought and worked hard to find a solution for one of the many problems that this scenario poses.

Using an arduino uno, we control a linear actuator and detect obstacles and position to help the wearer of the prosthetic live an enhanced life.
